PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: March 2026

Sota: Stream Entry Guide ("Sota", "the app") is built with a simple principle: your practice data belongs to you.

DATA WE COLLECT

Sota collects no personal data. The app does not require an account, does not track you, and does not send any data to our servers.

DATA STORED ON YOUR DEVICE

The following is stored locally on your device using your browser's localStorage:

  • Your practice log (session durations, dates, notes)
  • App settings and preferences
  • Your Anthropic API key (used only to make direct API calls from your device)
  • Your ElevenLabs API key, if provided (used only for voice synthesis)
  • Your resolution and retreat progress

None of this data leaves your device except as described below.

ANTHROPIC API

If you use the AI Guide feature, your conversation messages are sent directly from your device to Anthropic's API using your own API key. These requests are governed by Anthropic's Privacy Policy. Sota does not intercept, store, or have access to these messages.

ELEVENLABS API

If you enable voice synthesis, text is sent directly from your device to ElevenLabs using your own API key. These requests are governed by ElevenLabs' Privacy Policy. Sota does not intercept or store this data.

NOTIFICATIONS

If you enable daily reminders, notifications are generated locally on your device. No data is sent to any server.

THIRD-PARTY ANALYTICS

Sota contains no third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking of any kind.

CHILDREN

Sota is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.

CHANGES

If this policy changes materially, we will update the date above. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any changes.
